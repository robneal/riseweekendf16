What is Rise

RISE is the Rutgers Innovation & Startup Expedition, an overnight experience where creative and passionate students will bring their innovative ideas to life.

Students will formulate the first iteration of their product, market test it and present the results to esteemed investors and community leaders -- all in one weekend. Students from all backgrounds are welcome to apply.

Applications closed on Tuesday Nov 8th @ 11:59pm. We look forward to seeing you at the event on Saturday.

What We Do

Bootstrap

We'll teach you how to bootstrap your projects step by step using just the resources already available to you. Resourcefulness is an essential trait for any innovator.

Connect

A platform to connect with the best & brightest doers at Rutgers. You will have a unique opportunity to meet and work with passionate students looking to make an impact!

Educate

Through workshops from distinguished professionals, all program participants will learn the basic steps necessary to build out any project/idea they may pursue in the future.

Create

Within two days, you will have something tangible to show for your efforts. Whether it be a website, prototype or market research, you will have created something to take forward.

Brainstorm

Everyone will have the chance to explore a variety of creative solutions to problems they are interested in. They will learn both conventional and unconventional methods for creative ideation.

Synergy

Learn the quintessential business buzzwords intuitively designed to foster your personal brand. Here's a tip to get you started.
